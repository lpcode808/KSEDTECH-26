Learning Strands

CONNECT through culture, transformation, well-being, advocacy, and agency

CONNECT

Culture

Ground innovation in place, moʻolelo, ʻōlelo Hawaiʻi, ʻāina-based learning, and technologies of past and present.

CONNECT

Transformation

Advocacy

Agency
